About

The National Institutes of Science and Technology (INCT) are the result of a federal government program that has been fostering the networked work of the country's best research groups in strategic areas since the creation of the program in 2008. In 2022, the Department of Automation was accredited as the headquarters of the National Institute of Science and Technology (INCT) in Control and Automation of Energy Processes (CAPE).

INCT CAPE operates at the frontier of knowledge in the field of control and automation applied to energy processes, focusing on microgrids using renewable energy and green hydrogen, and on oil and natural gas production processes.
